What We Build

Every project starts as a plan worth standing behind.

From the first design conversation to the final walk-through, our crew handles the hardscape, the plantings, and everything that keeps it looking right for years after.

Design

Landscape Design

A full site plan built around how you actually use your yard — patios, beds, and sightlines mapped before a single shovel goes in.

Hardscape

Patios & Walls

Paver and natural stone patios, retaining walls, and walkways, set to drain right and hold up through every Ohio freeze-thaw cycle.

Planting

Garden & Planting Design

Beds layered for year-round color and structure, chosen for what actually thrives in Medina County soil and light.

Lighting

Outdoor Lighting

Low-voltage path, accent, and patio lighting that extends the evening and shows off the design after the sun goes down.

Grading

Drainage & Grading

Correcting standing water and erosion at the source, so the landscape above ground stays healthy below it.

Upkeep

Seasonal Maintenance

Bed cleanups, mulching, and pruning on a schedule, so the landscape we built keeps looking the way it did on day one.

How a Project Runs

Communication is half the job.

Clients tell us the same thing every time: they always knew what was happening and when. That's by design.

Step One

Walk & Listen

Jeremy walks the property with you, takes the site as it is, and listens to how you want to use the space before drawing anything.

Step Two

Design & Price

You get a real plan and a real number — materials, plant selections, and a schedule that accounts for Ohio's growing season.

Step Three

Build

The same crew that scoped the job builds it, with regular updates so there are no surprises mid-project.

Step Four

Walk-Through & Care

A final walk of the finished space, plus straightforward guidance on watering, pruning, and what to expect as it fills in.
